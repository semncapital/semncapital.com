Investment Preferences

Minnesota companies

We favor Minnesota-based companies, but preferably ones located in Southeast Minnesota or with a nexus to Mayo Clinic.

Innovation

We favor companies with innovative technologies and/or product ideas. We are industry agnostic, but invest mostly in the med tech and life science sectors.


Seed and early stage

We are angel investors who invest in seed and early stage rounds where equity capital is most scarce and opportunities for substantial gains are highest

Great entrepreneurs and founders

Above all, we back visionary founders and great teams. Strong leadership is the one common ingredient among our most successful portfolio companies
